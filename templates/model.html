What is the® System?

Brain tumors has always been a great challenge to both the doctors and the patients , but thanks to the researchers efforts it has been discovered that if the tumor has as specific genectic sequence ( MGMT promoter methylation ) the chances of the success of chemotheraoy and radiation increases significantly , but to know if the promoter exists or not , we have to a biopsy that can be very dangerous sometimes , so we have built a deep learning model to predict the existence of the said promoter using only MRI scans
